# QRDS Phase 131 - Evidence Quality Dimension Registry Research-Only

Gate: PHASE131_EVIDENCE_QUALITY_DIMENSION_REGISTRY_RESEARCH_ONLY_READY_RESEARCH_ONLY

Adds a research-only registry of evidence quality dimensions.

Dimensions:
- source traceability
- timestamp freshness
- gap integrity
- replay reproducibility
- review completeness

Boundary:
- evidence quality status is candidate research-only
- no edge validation
- no decision authority
- no trading signal
- no recommendation
- no allocation
- no safe-apply
- no promotion
- no canonical write
