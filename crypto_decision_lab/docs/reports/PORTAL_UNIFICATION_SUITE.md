# QRDS/QOS Portal Unification Suite

Sprint 9U consolidates the portal/documentation UX layer into one research-only launcher map.

It reconciles:

- dashboard and hub surfaces;
- validation/evidence/command-center surfaces;
- documentation/book/reader surfaces;
- data-foundation portals;
- serve wrappers and launcher candidates.

This is navigation only. It does not unlock operational use, does not connect to any account, does not generate orders, and does not produce executable instructions.

Acceptance criteria:

- the suite locates portal indexes;
- the suite locates documentation surfaces;
- at least three primary portal families are mapped;
- root serve wrappers are discoverable;
- policy lock remains `ACTIVE`;
- mode remains `INTERACTIVE_RESEARCH_ONLY`.
