# QRDS/QOS Phase 26 Regime-Segmented Volatility Edge Audit Pack

Sprint 26A–26R audits whether strengthened volatility baselines show candidate edge in specific regimes.

Definition in this project:

- edge is repeatable out-of-sample improvement over strong baselines;
- edge must survive regime, robustness, cost/slippage, and shadow/paper gates before it can become operational;
- Phase 26 can only identify research candidates, never operational edge.

Safety constraints:

- research candidates are not operational edge;
- no trading signal;
- no recommendation;
- no allocation;
- no operational decision;
- no safe-apply;
- no canonical promotion;
- zero canonical writes.
