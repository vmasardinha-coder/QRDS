# QRDS/QOS — Data Quality Gate v1

Sprint 9C adds a formal data-quality and reliability review packet for the QRDS/QOS evidence stack.

The gate is research-only. It records readability, lineage, safety consistency, data-coverage dependency, and missing quality audits. It cannot unlock execution, recommendations, orders, allocation, or live-fund workflows.

Expected result at this stage is incomplete or partial. The purpose is to make missing data-quality checks visible before any future research promotion.
